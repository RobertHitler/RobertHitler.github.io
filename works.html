Augmented reality

AR Architecture app

The Augmented reality (AR) app, this augments the 3D model of a building or an architecture in full-scale on a plane. The app built using Vuforia plane detection feature in unity. The app detects the plane and augments the full scale 3D model on the plane, this gives the user an experience of seeing and walking around the 3D model building, as if he walking around in a real house or building.
